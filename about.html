saiph

saiph is a 21 year old electronic artist, producer, & songwriter from Cleveland, OH. Born in 2005, saiph brings a lot of inspriation from childhood pop culture, not to mention childhood nostalgia being a "2010s kid". saiph first gained traction with his new jazz EP "memory" released in 2023. Since then, saiph has stuck to his roots in underground rap production, frequently releasing with friends like KHR Chance, Mundz, and as of more recently, Kynx. saiph's release "HAVEN" in 2026 has set a new tone for what world of his members of society may walk into.
